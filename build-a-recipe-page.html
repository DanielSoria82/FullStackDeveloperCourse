French Fries Recipe

a famous and delicious dish

Ingredients!!!!!

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Optional seasonings: Paprika, black pepper or Parmesan cheese
  • Cold water (for soaking)

Instructions

  1. Choose the potatoes: Use starchy potatoes like Russet or Yukon Gold for the best texture.
  2. Peel and cut: Peel the potatoes (optional) and cut them into thin, even strips.
  3. Soak in water: Place the cut potatoes in cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  4. Drain and dry: Drain the potatoes and pat them completely dry with a towel.
  5. Heat the oil: Use vegetable oil and heat it to around 160째C (320째F) in a deep pan or fryer.
  6. First fry: Fry the potatoes in batches for about 3-4 minutes, until soft but not golden. Remove and drain on paper towels
  7. Increase the heat: Raise the oil temperature to 190째C (375째F).
  8. Second fry: Fry the potatoes again for 2-3 minutes, until golden and crispy.
  9. Drain and season: Remove from the oil, drain on paper towels, and sprinkle with salt (or your favorite seasoning).
  10. Serve and enjoy: Serve hot with ketchup, mayonnaise, or any dipping sauce you like!
